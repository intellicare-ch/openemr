# Care Coordination

**Capability ID:** 14
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-15

## Overview
Care coordination tools including care plans, care teams, referrals, transition of care documents, health information exchange, goals tracking, and multi-provider collaboration.

## Key Features
- Care plan creation and management
- Care team assignment
- Referral tracking and management
- Transition of care documents (CCDA)
- Health information exchange integration
- Patient goals documentation
- Care plan interventions
- Multi-provider collaboration
- Encounter CCD dispatch

**Module:** `/interface/modules/zend_modules/module/Carecoordination/`
**Files:** Care plan forms, referral management
**Tables:** Care plan related tables
**Standards:** CCDA for transitions of care

## Configuration
- Care plan templates
- Referral workflows
- HIE connectivity
- CCD dispatch settings
