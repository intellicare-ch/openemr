# Therapy Groups

**Capability ID:** 21
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-15

## Overview
Group therapy management for behavioral health including group session scheduling, attendance tracking, group encounter documentation, and group billing.

## Key Features
- Group therapy session creation
- Group participant management
- Group scheduling
- Attendance tracking
- Group encounter documentation
- Group progress notes
- Group billing (group therapy CPT codes)
- Participant history tracking
- Group session templates

**Files:** `/interface/therapy_groups/`, `/interface/forms/group_attendance/`, `/interface/forms/newGroupEncounter/`
**Tables:** `therapy_groups`, `therapy_groups_participants`, `therapy_groups_participant_attendance`

## Configuration
- Group therapy types
- Group capacity limits
- Billing codes for group therapy

**End of Therapy Groups Capability**
