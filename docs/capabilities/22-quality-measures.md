# Quality Measures and Reporting

**Capability ID:** 22
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-15

## Overview
Clinical quality measure (CQM) calculation and reporting, meaningful use attestation, automated measure calculation (AMC), public health reporting, and registry submissions.

## Key Features
- Clinical Quality Measures (CQM) - 20+ NQF measures
- Automated Measure Calculation (AMC) - Meaningful Use tracking
- Quality measure reporting engine
- Population identification (denominator, numerator, exclusions)
- FHIR-based measure definitions
- MIPS reporting support
- HEDIS measure support
- Public health reporting
- Registry submission capability
- Measure trending and dashboards
- Custom quality measures

**Files:** `/library/classes/rulesets/Cqm/`, `/library/classes/rulesets/Amc/`, `/interface/reports/cqm.php`, `/interface/reports/amc_tracking.php`

## Measures Include
- NQF-0002: Depression screening
- NQF-0013: Hypertension BP control
- NQF-0028: Tobacco screening
- NQF-0038: Childhood immunization
- NQF-0041: Preventive care screening
- Many more...
