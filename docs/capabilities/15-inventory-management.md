# Inventory Management

**Capability ID:** 15
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-15

## Overview
Medical inventory tracking for medications and supplies including drug inventory, lot tracking, expiration monitoring, sales tracking, and controlled substance documentation.

## Key Features
- Drug inventory management
- Lot number tracking
- Expiration date monitoring
- Inventory transactions (add, dispense, adjust, destroy)
- Low stock alerts
- Sales tracking and reporting
- Controlled substance logging (DEA compliance)
- Destruction documentation
- Inventory valuation
- Barcode support

**Files:** `/interface/drugs/`
**Tables:** `drug_inventory`, `drug_sales`, `drug_templates`
**Reports:** `/interface/reports/inventory_*.php`

## Configuration
- Inventory tracking enabled/disabled
- Reorder thresholds
- DEA tracking requirements
- Pricing and markup

**End of Inventory Management Capability**
