# Therapy Groups - Requirements Specification
**Capability ID:** 21 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Last Updated:** 2025-11-15

## Functional Requirements
### FR-TG-001: Group Creation
**Description:** System shall support therapy group creation.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Group name and description | Capacity limits | Group type (open, closed) | Start/end dates | Facilitator assignment | Group schedule

### FR-TG-002: Participant Management
**Description:** System shall manage group participants.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Add patients to group | Remove participants | Participant status (active, inactive, waitlist) | Enrollment dates | Discharge dates and reasons

### FR-TG-003: Group Scheduling
**Description:** Group sessions shall integrate with calendar.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Recurring group sessions | Group appointment on calendar | Participant list visible | Session reminders to all participants

### FR-TG-004: Attendance Tracking
**Description:** System shall track session attendance.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Attendance form per session | Mark present/absent/excused | Attendance rate calculation | Attendance reports | Notification for poor attendance

### FR-TG-005: Group Encounter Documentation
**Description:** Group sessions shall be documented.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Group encounter creation | Group progress notes | Individual participant notes | Group topics/themes | Facilitator signature

### FR-TG-006: Group Billing
**Description:** Group therapy shall be billable.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Group therapy CPT codes (90853, etc.) | Bill all participants | Attendance affects billing | Group claim generation | Insurance coverage verification

## Non-Functional Requirements
### NFR-TG-001: Privacy
**Description:** Group participation shall be confidential.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Participant lists restricted access | Group notes separated from individual charts (optional) | Consent for group therapy

### NFR-TG-002: Reporting
**Description:** Group data shall be reportable.
**Acceptance Criteria:** Group census reports | Attendance summaries | Outcome tracking | Billing reports
